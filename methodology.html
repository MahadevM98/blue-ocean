Methodology

We don't brainstorm. We derive.

Every framework we build starts in the literature. We read what your competitors don't, then translate it into positions they cannot easily copy.

Powered by 200+ peer-reviewed papers
Quantum physics

Superposition of market positions

Before committing, a brand can occupy several potential positions simultaneously. We model that space of possibilities and identify which one, when observed by the market, yields the strongest and least contested outcome.

Drawn from work on superposition and measurement in quantum mechanics — applied as a decision framework, not a literal physics claim.

Behavioural economics

Predicting the irrational

Customers deviate from rational choice in patterned, measurable ways. We map the heuristics and biases at play in your category and design messaging and pricing that align with how decisions are actually made.

Grounded in prospect theory and the broader judgement-under-uncertainty literature.

Game theory

Finding the position no one will contest

We treat your market as a game with players, payoffs and moves. By modelling likely competitor responses, we locate positions that are stable — places where rivals cannot profitably follow you.

Built on equilibrium analysis and the economics of strategic interaction.

Synthesis

One coherent strategy

The disciplines don't run in parallel — they compound. Possibility space narrows through behavioural insight, then locks into a position made defensible by game theory. The result is a single, mathematically sound route to open water.

Every engagement ships with its source reading list, so you can check our work.
